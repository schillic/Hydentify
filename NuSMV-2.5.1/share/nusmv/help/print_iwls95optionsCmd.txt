  print_iwls95options - Prints the Iwls95 Options.

   --------------------------------------------------------------------------

   print_iwls95options [-h]

   This command prints out the configuration parameters of the IWLS95
   clustering algorithm, i.e. image_verbosity, image_cluster_size and
   image_W{1,2,3,4}.
